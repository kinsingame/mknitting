How I made the Coasters

The pattern I followed is available here

All credits to Ashleigh on sewrella.com for creating this pattern.

First, I create a slip knot.



Then, I creake a magic ring from it with double crochets and chains in the middle.



After that, I crochet into the chains with lots of 2 double crochet, and chains inbetween.



Same again.



Finally, I crochet into the chains with lots of 4 double crochet to create the flowery effect.
