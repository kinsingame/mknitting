How I made the Octopus

The pattern I followed is available here

All credits to Kara on khookcreations.com for creating this pattern.

First I made a magic ring with single crochet stitches.



I then expanded the circle by spacing out stitches where I do 2 single crochets.



I worked the body like tube by crocheting every stitch only once.



For every tentacle, I started by making a chain.



After that, I started doing smaller crochet stitches, then longer ones to shape them. I then slip stitched to the body.
