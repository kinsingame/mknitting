How I made the Rabbit

The pattern I followed is available here

First, I casted on a reasonable amount of stitches, ready to make into a square.



Because at this point I was just learning, I kept dropping stitches. I used the crochet needle to stitch it back up, and put it back on the needle.
I did this in stocking stitch, which is a repeat of 'knit a row, purl a row'.



After I finished the square, I sewed a triangle in the top half of it.



I then put the stuffing over and enclosed it as I pulled the triangle tight, to create the head.
